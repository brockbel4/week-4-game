Gem Counter!

  1. You will be provided with a random number upon the start of each game.
  2. There are four crystals below. When you click each one they will add a specific number of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided at the beginning of the game. You lose if your score becomes greater than the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  5. Each time a new game starts it will change the values of each crystal.
